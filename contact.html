Frequently Asked Questions

Check if your question has already been answered below:

How does CompressPhotos work?

CompressPhotos uses advanced JavaScript algorithms to compress images directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Is CompressPhotos really free?

Yes! CompressPhotos is completely free to use with no hidden fees, registration requirements, or usage limits. We're supported by non-intrusive advertising.

What image formats are supported?

We currently support JPEG/JPG images for compression. We're working on adding support for PNG, WebP, and other formats in future updates.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No! All image processing happens locally in your browser. We never upload, store, or have access to your images. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I use compressed images commercially?

Yes, you can use your compressed images for any purpose, including commercial use. You retain all rights to your images.

How do I report a bug or issue?

Please email us at bugs@compressphotos.cloud with details about the issue, including your browser version and steps to reproduce the problem.
