Frequently Asked Questions - Image Compressor FAQ

Get answers to common questions about our free image compressor and photo optimization tool

How much can I compress my images and reduce photo file size?

With our image compressor, you can typically reduce photo file sizes by 60-90% while maintaining excellent visual quality, depending on the original image format and your compression quality settings. Our advanced algorithms analyze each pixel to find the optimal balance between file size and image quality.

Is there a file size limit?

You can upload files up to 10MB each for optimal performance and processing speed. This limit ensures fast compression times while accommodating most photography needs, from social media posts to professional portfolio images.

What image formats does the photo compressor support?

Currently, our image compressor supports JPEG/JPG photo formats, which are the most common image types for photography and web use. Support for PNG compression, WebP optimization, HEIC conversion, and other image formats is coming soon based on user demand.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all compression happens directly in your browser using client-side processing. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, transmitted over the internet, or stored anywhere outside your device, ensuring complete privacy and data security.

How does image compression work?

Our tool uses advanced lossy and lossless compression algorithms to reduce file size. Lossy compression removes data that's less noticeable to the human eye, while lossless compression reorganizes data more efficiently without removing any information.

Can I batch compress multiple images at once?

Yes! Our batch compression feature allows you to upload and compress multiple images simultaneously. This saves time when processing large numbers of photos for websites, social media, or digital portfolios.

Will compression affect image quality?

Our intelligent compression maintains visual quality while reducing file size. You can adjust quality settings from 10% to 100% to find the perfect balance for your needs. Higher quality settings preserve more detail but result in larger files.

Is the image compressor free to use?

Yes, CompressPhotos is completely free to use with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or usage limits. We believe everyone should have access to professional-quality image compression tools without cost barriers.

How do I choose the right compression quality?

For web use, 70-80% quality typically provides the best balance. For printing, use 90-95%. For storage/backup, 60-70% is often sufficient. Our presets (Best Quality, Web Optimized, High Compression) make this choice easier.

Can I compress images on mobile devices?

Absolutely! CompressPhotos is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets. The touch-friendly interface makes it easy to drag and drop images on any mobile device.

Image Compression Tips and Best Practices

Professional advice for getting the best results from image compression

Web Optimization

For websites, aim for images under 100KB. Use 80% quality for most web images, and consider resizing large images before compression for even better file size reduction.

Mobile Performance

Mobile users have limited bandwidth. Compress images to 50-70% quality for mobile-first websites to ensure fast loading times and better user experience.

Print Quality

For printed materials, maintain 90-95% quality to preserve detail. Consider the print size - smaller prints can use more compression than large format prints.

Social Media

Different platforms have different requirements. Instagram: 1080x1080px at 75% quality. Facebook: up to 2048px width at 85% quality for best results.
