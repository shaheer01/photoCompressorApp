How much can I compress my images and reduce photo file size?

With our image compressor, you can typically reduce photo file sizes by 60-90% while maintaining excellent visual quality, depending on the original image format and your compression quality settings.

Is there a file size limit?

You can upload files up to 10MB each for optimal performance and processing speed.

What image formats does the photo compressor support?

Currently, our image compressor supports JPEG/JPG photo formats. Support for PNG compression, WebP optimization, and other image formats is coming soon.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all compression happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
