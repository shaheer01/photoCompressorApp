Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our JPEG compression tool

How much can I compress my images?

Typically, you can reduce file sizes by 60-90% while maintaining excellent visual quality, depending on the original image and your quality settings.

Is there a file size limit?

Free users can upload files up to 10MB each. Premium users enjoy unlimited file sizes and batch processing.

What formats are supported?

Currently, we support JPEG/JPG formats. Support for PNG, WebP, and other formats is coming soon.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all compression happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
